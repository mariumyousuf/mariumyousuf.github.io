Welcome to My Webpage

I am a PhD student in the Program of Applied Mathematics, Department of Mathematics, at the University of Arizona. I am interested in probabilistic graphical model applications to computational neuroscience.

For my dissertation, I am investigating causal discovery techniques to extract functional connectivity in neurons under the implication of hippocampal replay. See the Research tab for more.
